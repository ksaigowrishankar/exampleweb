Semantic vs Non-Semantic Elements in HTML

Non-semantic elements → <div> and <span>
They have no meaning. They are just containers for grouping or styling.

Semantic elements → <header>, <nav>, <main>, <article>, etc.
They clearly describe the meaning and purpose of the content.
